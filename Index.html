Baba Dham Mandir

The Baba Dham temple is situated in Jamalpura, Sonepat. Baba Dham has two big statues of Lord Shiva and Lord Hanuman and is frequented by many pilgrims.

Khatu Shyam Mandir

Sonipat Dham is also know for Khatu shyam dham. This is a temple build recently. You can sit and do meditation, pray without any disturbance. Along with Khatu Shyam baba , Radha Krishna and lord Hanuman ji statue are also there.
